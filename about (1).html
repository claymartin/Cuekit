About Cue Kit

Cue Kit is a mental training tool designed for athletes to build confidence, manage anxiety, and maintain focus during performance. Using evidence-based techniques like breathing exercises, visualization, and positive self-talk, Cue Kit helps you develop personalized cue plans that fit your needs.

How It Works

Track your mental state in the journal, answer a few questions, and get a customized plan with cues and routines to use on the court. Color-coded cues on your shoe straps serve as quick reminders to stay in the zone.

Why Cue Kit?

  • Personalized to your current challenges
  • Based on sports psychology research
  • Easy to implement with minimal equipment
